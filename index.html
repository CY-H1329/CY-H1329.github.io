Research
LLM / VLM Spatial Reasoning Multi-Agent Systems World Models Embodied AI

My research focuses on bridging high-level vision and physical reasoning by advancing spatial understanding as a core intelligence for Embodied AI. I am particularly interested in how large language and vision-language models can develop robust spatial representations, how multi-agent frameworks can collaborate and self-improve, and how world models provide a foundation for grounded planning across diverse environments.
